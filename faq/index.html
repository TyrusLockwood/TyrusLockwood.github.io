FAQ

  • How do I cancel groups after enabling auto-grouping?

    Click the "Auto" button at the bottom left of the sidepanel list to turn off the auto-grouping feature and cancel all current groups.

  • After enabling auto-grouping, there are too many groups. How can I group more effectively?

    Click the "Settings" button at the bottom right of the sidepanel list. You can configure custom rules in the "Grouping Rules" section. Don’t forget to click the "Apply" button after configuration.

  • Do I have to enable auto-grouping to use the full features?

    No. Auto-grouping is just one of Tabbiy’s core features. Other features like the sidepanel list, tab saving, tab hibernation, and quick switching can all be used independently.

  • How do I adjust the position of the sidepanel?

    You need to go to Chrome’s Settings → Appearance → sidepanel Position option.

  • Why can’t groups in the sidepanel list be collapsed?

    The groups in the list are not designed to be collapsible. This is to keep all open tabs visible at a glance, making it faster to find the tab you need.

  • The sidepanel list is too wide and takes up too much screen space.

    The current width is already the minimum for Chrome’s sidepanel. For now, you can use shortcuts or enable the "auto-close when mouse moves away" feature to reduce screen space usage.

  • Are saved tabs only temporarily stored?

    Saved tabs will be displayed in the staging list and stored long-term unless manually cleared.

  • Are settings syncable?

    Yes, user settings are synced. However, saved tabs are not synced and are only stored locally.

  • Does Tabbiy support other browsers?

    Currently, Tabbiy only supports Chrome. Due to differences in APIs and extension mechanisms, it is not compatible with other browsers.
